The enterprise reality

Complexity compounds.
Systems don't keep up.

Global enterprises operate systems that were never designed to work together — SAP for trade compliance, Oracle for finance, Salesforce for CRM, custom platforms for operations, BizTalk for integration. Each system does its job. None of them perform as a whole. The gaps between them are where performance goes to die.

Captiv's enterprise practice is built around one principle: make the systems you already have perform. Not replacement. Not rip-and-replace. Integration, automation and intelligence on top of what exists — deployed by teams who own the outcome.
