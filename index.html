Our platforms

Six engines.
One purpose.

Each engine represents a critical layer of how institutions function — from cloud infrastructure through operations, governance and intelligence. Every layer depends on the one below it. Together they form the only complete institutional performance stack of its kind.

Layer 5 · Intelligence QLens
Intelligence & decision systems
When every layer below is running, QLens tells you what to improve next. It sits across your entire system stack — surfacing inefficiencies, predicting failures, and converting operational data into decisions before problems become crises.
AI · ML Decision intelligence IDP · OCR ERP AI layer Performance command centre
Layer 4 · Governance SonicLynk
Compliance, control & surveillance
Institutions that handle public funds, sovereign data, and citizen services need more than software — they need accountability built into the system. SonicLynk enforces compliance at the point of action, not after the fact, making governance the operating layer rather than an audit function.
Smart city eGovernance Safe city Port systems Defence Fund monitoring
Layer 3 · Operational & Financial systems Optima 360·AION
Optima 360 — Operations
Most organisations have processes. Few have systems that enforce them. Optima 360 turns every approval, transaction and workflow into a governed, measurable event — eliminating the gap between policy and what actually happens on the ground.
BPM · Low-code P2P · O2C AP automation Service lifecycle
AION — Financial systems
Banks and financial institutions don't fail because of bad strategy — they fail because their systems can't keep up with the volume, complexity and regulatory weight of real operations. AION is built for the full lifecycle of money movement, from origination to collections to compliance, across borders and currencies.
Loan origination Collections Trade finance SWIFT GRC
Layer 2 · Execution & delivery ProAug
Embedded execution & sustained performance
Systems degrade without the right people sustaining them. ProAug places Captiv's own engineers inside your organisation — not as contractors filling seats, but as accountable delivery owners who understand your systems end-to-end and keep performance from drifting over time.
Embedded teams Continuous delivery Outcome ownership Specialist resources
Layer 1 · Foundation SkyVerse
Cloud & infrastructure
Every system Captiv builds runs on infrastructure that must never fail. SkyVerse doesn't just procure cloud — it architects environments where performance is engineered into every layer of compute, storage and network, with cost discipline built in from day one rather than retrofitted later.
AWS · Azure · GCP GPU infra FinOps Managed cloud Multi-AZ HA

Captiv's approach is about adopting systems and processes to improve efficiency at each layer — building up from infrastructure, through operations and governance, to intelligence that compounds value across the entire stack.
